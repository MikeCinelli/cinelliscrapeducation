Chess

How I teach it

The first thing a student needs to know are the rules of chess. After that, it's my job to make them improve as optimally as possible. The best things for chess improvement are: playing games, studying great players of the past, and solving chess exercises. I allow students to play me online whenever they want, I play on my phone so I can basically always respond to games. On a similar note, I believe that community is very important to chess so I will start and run an online chess club. The ultimate goal is to create and run an in person club, "The Wood-Ridge Chess Club."
