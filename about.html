About Me

I am a lifelong Wood-Ridge resident. My parents are lifelong Wood-Ridge residents. My best friends are from Wood-Ridge. I started playing piano at nine, chess at ten and I studied math at college. I want my job to be teaching people my favorite things.

Teaching Experience

I was the full time chess teacher for Success Academy Charter Schools Hell's Kitchen. As a full time chess teacher I taught over 150 students grades K-4. I managed three afterschool clubs including a 30 student travelling club. I have been tutoring piano around Bergen County for around a decade. I currently am a music director at School of Rock Carlstadt.
