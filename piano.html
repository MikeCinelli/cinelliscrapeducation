Piano

How I teach it

The first thing I teach is "how to play the piano." The student learns posture, hand placement and the proper way to use their fingers. The goal is to build the best possible habits as early as possible. Soon, our technique will be put into practice as we learn our first song. The song is easy, instructive and sounds great. After this we begin a cycle of playing: songs the student wants to learn, classical masterpieces, and musical exercises. The exercises teach fundamentals, music theory and how to read music. Classical masterpieces are longterm projects, personalized songbooks keeps the student engaged. The idea is to keep students in a flow state; challenged but not overwhelmed.
